Teaching


I'm a Head Teaching Assistant for CIS 110 at Penn, the University of Pennsylvania's Introductory Computer Programming course. I'm incredibly passionate about making computer science a more welcoming environment for students like me who were afraid of the field before coming to college and for studenets from marginalized backgrounds.

Some of the work we do in my class involve making recursive graphics, cracking the Caesar Cipher, creating a guitar out of your keyboard, and implementing some heuristics for the Traveling Salesperson Problem.
